Our Story

Building excellence since 1990

BuildMaster's roots can be traced back to 1990 when the founding team started its small, local construction company. BuildMaster has since grown to be one of the leading construction and engineering firms across the country.

1990

BuildMaster Construction was founded as a small residential contractor with just 5 employees.

1995

Completed our first major commercial project, a 10-story office building in the downtown area.

2005

Expanded operations to include infrastructure projects, opening offices in three additional states.

2010

Pioneered sustainable construction practices, earning our first LEED Platinum certification.

2023

Today, BuildMaster employs over 250 professionals and has completed more than 500 projects nationwide.
